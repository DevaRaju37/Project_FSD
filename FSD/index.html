About the webpage

SwachhConnect is a smart waste collection management platform for both rural and urban areas. This platform helps citizens report waste by uploading images/videos along with location and details of the waste. It directly connects to nearby municipal authorities for quick action.

Why Clean Surroundings Matter?

  • Prevents diseases and improves public health
  • Protects the environment and wildlife
  • Creates a positive and hygienic living space
  • Supports the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

How to Keep Your Surroundings Clean

  • Avoid littering in public places
  • Report unmanaged waste through this platform

Instructions to Report Waste

  • Click on the Image button below
  • Upload a clear image or video of the waste
  • Describe the type and location of the waste
  • Submit the report for municipal action
